Angie Klein

Mrs. Klein has a MBA from Rockhurst University in Entrepreneurship, a Bachelor from Missouri State in Computer and Information Systems and Teaching Certificate from Northwest Missouri State. She has worked in industry as a Systems Engineer and Information Technology Auditor. In industry, she worked for Cerner Corporation, DST Systems, Kansas City Southern and Kansas City Power and Light. After leaving industry, Mrs. Klein was an Assistant Professor in Computer Science at Park University and William Jewell College. Her experience in technology has led her to teaching technology here at Liberty. She teaches Computer Programming, AP Computer Science, Web Programming and Computer Architecture. Mrs. Klein also enjoys teaching Entrepreneurship where she uses her entrepreneurial experience from running her own web based business to excite students in becoming future entrepreneurial leaders.
